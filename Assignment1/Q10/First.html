Churlish :

Meaning : An adjective used to describe a rude, boorish, or miserly person, it takes its origins from 9th-century England, where a cherl or ceorl was a title given to a freeman of the lowest rank

Usage :

  1. It seems churlish to criticise such a major achievement.
  2. It seems churlish to criticise this book, since it is brimming over with good stuff.
  3. It would be churlish to pick fault at these levels.

Frisson :

Meaning :A sudden, passing shudder of emotion or excitement, frisson originates from the Latin frictio, meaning to shiver and as a derivative of frigere, meaning t o be cold; related to the word “friction”

Usage :

  1. "I always feel a slight frisson when I cross over to the south."
  2. "The game has an added frisson because of the opposition."
  3. "a frisson of excitement"
